Overview: The workshop will consist of talks that are aimed towards a broad audience and designed to foster discussion and interaction between the CS and IT communities. That is, workshop talks will not be limited to the four primary themes, any topic at the interface of IT and CS is of interest. To encourage broad participation from both communities, there will be no proceedings and talks of a survey nature are highly encouraged.

  • Location: Amphitheater Hermite, Institut Henri Poincaré, 11 Rue Pierre et Marie Curie, 75005 Paris, France (Google Maps).

  • There will be a Cocktail and Dinner Reception from 18:00 - 21:00 on Thu. Mar. 3rd. The reception will take place on the 24th floor of the Zamansky Tower on the Pierre and Marie Curie University Campus, 4 Place Jussieu, 75005 Paris, France (Google Maps), which is a 15-minute walk from IHP. You will need your passport or ID card in order to enter the building.
