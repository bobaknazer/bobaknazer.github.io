IHP Spring 2016 Thematic Program

How to Participate

There are many ways to get involved in the IHP Program. Unless otherwise specified, all events take place at IHP in Paris, France.

Learn:

  • The tutorial week at CIRM in Marseille, France will provide a broad overview of each of the major themes through a series of tutorials.

  • Mini-courses are planned during each of the two-week themes, Distributed Computation and Communication, Fundamental Inequalities and Lower Bounds, Inference Problems, and Secrecy and Privacy.

  • Talks at the central workshop are aimed towards a broad audience.

Speak:

  • The deadline for central workshop talk submissions has passed. For last-minute arrangements, please contact the organizers.

Discuss:

  • The primary themes and central workshop will provide many opportunities for discussion and collaboration.

  • The program as a whole is designed to foster discussion and collaboration between the IT and CS communities on problems of mutual interest.

How to Register

Attendance is open to all and free for any part of the school, primary themes, and workshop (subject to space limitations). All that is required is a quick registration using the appropriate link below.
